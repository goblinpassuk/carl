🔐 YubiKey Encrypted Notes (WebAuthn + AES-256)

💡 Ready - Register your YubiKey to begin
⚡ How it works:
1. Click "Register" and touch your YubiKey when prompted
2. After registration, click "Unlock" and touch again
3. Your notes are encrypted with AES-256-GCM using a key derived from your YubiKey
4. Data is stored encrypted in your browser (localStorage)
5. Only your YubiKey can decrypt the data
